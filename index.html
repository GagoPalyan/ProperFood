I'm Debbie; a nutritionist, recipe developer, wife, mum and home cook. Properfoodie is my recipe website where I share nutritious, family friendly meals, designed to fit in with every day, busy lives! Email me at: properfood@gmail.com

This blog was a tiny seed that was planted a long time ago and now finally I am taking a big step forward to begin. I have always loved to cook but there were times when I haven’t always cooked. Over the years as I began to cook more and more there was an overwhelming feeling of joy through trying new recipes. This is a space for me to share the food that I create.
